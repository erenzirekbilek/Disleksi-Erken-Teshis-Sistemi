## Purpose of the Study

The Dyslexia Early Detection System is a research project designed to develop an AI-powered tool that can help identify early signs of dyslexia in students through analysis of speech, handwriting, and text samples. Early detection can lead to timely intervention and support.

---

## What is Involved?

If you consent to your child's participation, your child will be asked to:

1. **Speech Recording:** Read aloud from a standardized passage (3-5 minutes)
2. **Handwriting Sample:** Complete a handwriting task (5-10 minutes)
3. **Text Writing:** Write a short response to a prompt (5-10 minutes)

All activities will be conducted at school under supervision.

---

## Risks and Benefits

**Risks:** There are no known risks associated with this study. The activities are similar to regular classroom tasks.

**Benefits:** Your child may benefit from early identification of dyslexia risk factors. You will receive a summary of findings (if clinically significant) to share with your child's teacher or healthcare provider.
